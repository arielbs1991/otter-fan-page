Otters are My Husband's Favorite Animal

He thinks they are absolutely adorable.

Otters also have the capacity to be incredibly violent. They have been known to murder baby seals, keeping the bodies for days to rape until they are too decomposed to continue doing so.

They do hold hands when they sleep as to not float away from each other, so that's pretty cute.

And they use rocks placed on their chests to break open clams, which is also pretty cute.
